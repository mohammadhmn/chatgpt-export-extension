Privacy

ChatGPT Chat Exporter

Summary

This extension exports chat content from the ChatGPT website to files on your device.

Data Collection

The extension does not collect, transmit, sell, or share your data. It does not use analytics or tracking.

How Data Is Used

Your chat content is read only from the currently open ChatGPT page in your browser when you click Export. Files are generated locally and downloaded using your browser.

Permissions

activeTab/scripting are used to run the exporter in the active ChatGPT tab when you click the extension. storage is used to save your settings.
